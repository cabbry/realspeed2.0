Privacy Policy for RealSpeed2

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Thank you for using RealSpeed2 ("the App"). Your privacy matters to us. This document explains what data the App handles and what it does not.

Summary

RealSpeed2 does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data with us, our servers, or any third party. Everything happens locally on your device.

Location Data

The App uses your device's GPS to compute your real-time speed.

  • Location data is read only while the App is in use (foreground).
  • Location data is never transmitted off your device.
  • Location data is never stored in any persistent file controlled by us.
  • iOS asks for your permission before the App accesses your location. You can revoke this permission at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → RealSpeed2.

Stored Data

The App stores one value on your device, locally:

  • Maximum recorded speed (Vmax), used to display your personal best between launches. This value never leaves your device. You can reset it at any time from inside the App using the "Reset Vmax" button, or by uninstalling the App.

Third-party services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking SDKs.

Children's privacy

The App is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at the same URL with an updated "Last updated" date.
